### 1. Information We Collect
We do not collect any personal information unless you voluntarily provide it or unless it is required for the specific functionality of the App you are using.

#### 1.1 Camera & Microphone (e.g., MathPick)
- **Usage:** To capture images of math problems or convert speech to text.
- **Storage:** Images and audio are processed locally or sent to our AI service provider for immediate analysis and are NOT permanently stored on our servers.

#### 1.2 User Input Data (e.g., KAIRIS)
For Apps providing fortune-telling or tarot reading, we process the text data you input (e.g., questions, concerns).
- **Usage:** To generate AI-based advice or results.
- **Privacy:** Input data is processed anonymously, sent to the AI model securely, and is not used to identify any individual.

#### 1.3 Financial & Ledger Data (Future Services)
For business management or freight ledger Apps, numerical data is stored locally on your device unless you explicitly opt-in to cloud backup features.

### 2. Personal Data
We do not knowingly collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, residential address, or phone number.

### 3. Third-Party Services
We use trusted third-party services to improve our Apps and generate revenue.
- **Google AdMob:** Used to display advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers (Advertising ID) to serve personalized ads.
- **Google Firebase:** Used for app stability analysis and secure backend services (App Check).
- **Google Generative AI (Gemini):** Used to process complex queries. Data sent to the AI model is anonymous and is not used for further training.

### 4. Children's Privacy
Our Apps are intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
